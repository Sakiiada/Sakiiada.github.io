Shiki is from Kara no kyoukai created by Kiyoko Nasu. Shiki Ryougi (両儀式, Ryōgi Shiki) is the protagonist of Kara no Kyoukai and the bearer of the Mystic Eyes of Death Perception, allowing her to see death and bring it upon anything "alive" for as long as she can run her knife across its Lines of Death. Hailing from a family of demon hunters, she developed two personalities as per her family's custom. A cold, feminine personality is referred to as "Shiki" and a cheerful, masculine personality called "SHIKI." But she lost SHIKI after a suicide attempt left her in a coma for two years.
